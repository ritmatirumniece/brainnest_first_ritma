My job

I have been teacher for more than 30 years and I could say this is the most wonderful profession in the world. Yoy always feel young and do different interesting things together with your students.

I had different duties at my work:

  • I was a teacher;
  • I was a teacher- trainer;
  • I was an international project coordinator;
  • I was a new program (IB MYP)coordinator.

I haven't count how many students I had been taught. Wherever I go, I meet them everywhere. In summers I use to organize parties with my graduates.
